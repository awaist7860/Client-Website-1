About the owner

I’m Saira Urooj, Director and Lead Physiotherapist at SU Therapies, with over five years’ specialist experience in Women’s Health and Pelvic Health Physiotherapy. I’m HCPC registered and a member of the CSP and POGP.

I specialise in pelvic floor dysfunction, including incontinence, prolapse, pelvic pain, sexual dysfunction, and pregnancy- and menopause-related conditions. As an Advanced Intimate Aesthetician, I offer an integrated, evidence-based approach that supports both function and confidence.
