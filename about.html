About

Compassionate, confidential, evidence-based women’s health care.

Our approach combines education, hands-on therapy where appropriate, and personalised exercise programmes. We focus on restoring function, improving comfort, and supporting long-term confidence.

Areas we support

  • Pelvic floor and continence concerns
  • Pregnancy and postnatal recovery
  • Menopause and hormonal changes
  • Scar rehabilitation and post-surgical recovery
  • Pessary fitting and follow-up care
